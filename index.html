Research

My research focuses on the design and foundations of programming languages, and my main interest is programming
languages semantics. I mainly work with Elena Zucca, Davide Ancona and Francesco Dagnino.

My main interests

  • Non-well-founded data structures
  • Operational semantics and operational reasoning
  • Coinductive reasoning and corecursion
  • Regular terms
